# LinkedIn Authority Post

## Title
AI Is About to Reshape How Professional Services Firms Create Leverage

## Source Memo
Houston Professional Services AI Operating Model

## Industry
Professional Services

## Post
Professional services firms have always operated on a leverage model.

Junior staff perform research and preparation.

Senior advisors focus on judgment, relationships, and strategy.

AI introduces a new layer of leverage inside that structure.

But many firms are approaching it incorrectly.

Across consulting, advisory, and professional services firms I'm seeing the same pattern:

• individuals experimenting with AI tools
• teams creating isolated prompt workflows
• knowledge bases copied into different systems

This creates fragmented adoption.

The firms that win with AI will not be the ones using the most tools.

They will be the ones that institutionalize AI into their operating model.

Not as a tool.

As a capability.

Houston's professional services ecosystem—especially around energy and healthcare—has a unique opportunity to lead here.
