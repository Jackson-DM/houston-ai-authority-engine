# CRM Signal Tracker

Manual tracking template for inbound signals from content and outreach.
One entry per contact. Duplicate the entry block as needed.

---

## Signal Strength Rubric

| Score | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| 1 | Passive engagement — like, view, or follow with no context |
| 2 | Light signal — comment, share, or profile visit after post |
| 3 | Direct signal — reply, DM, or unsolicited question |
| 4 | Qualified signal — expressed a specific problem or asked for a conversation |
| 5 | High-intent — requested a call, introduced themselves with context, or made a referral |

---

## Status Options

- `New` — signal received, not yet reviewed
- `Reviewed` — assessed, no action taken
- `Nurture` — worth watching; no immediate action
- `Active` — in conversation
- `Scheduled` — call or meeting booked
- `Closed — Won` — converted to client or referral partner
- `Closed — Lost` — no fit or contact went cold
- `On Hold` — paused at their request or pending external factor
