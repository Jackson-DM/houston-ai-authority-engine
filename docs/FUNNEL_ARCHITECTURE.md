# FUNNEL ARCHITECTURE
## Houston AI Authority Engine → Enterprise Pipeline

Purpose: Convert Houston AI Club visibility + LinkedIn authority into enterprise retainers. This funnel must be:
- Structured
- Predictable
- Repeatable
- Measurable

---

# OVERALL FLOW
Community Visibility → Executive Identification → Private Executive Engagement → Paid Diagnostic → 90-Day Retainer → Long-Term Expansion

---

# STAGE 1: Visibility Layer
Sources:
- Houston AI Club meetups
- Lightning Lessons
- LinkedIn content
- Executive memos
- Press features
Goal: Identify senior leaders in attendance or engagement.
Key Question: Who in this audience controls budget?

---

# STAGE 2: Executive Identification
Mechanisms:
- RSVP segmentation (role-based question)
- LinkedIn event attendee review
- Manual tagging in CRM (future)

RSVP Question Example: "What best describes your role?"
- Executive / VP / Director
- Operator / Manager
- Engineer / Builder
- Student / Other

Only executives enter next stage automatically.

---

# STAGE 3: Executive Follow-Up
Within 48 hours post-event:
Send: Houston Executive AI Brief (1-page insight summary)
Offer: Invitation to monthly Executive AI Office Hours (private, invite-only)
Goal: Move from public community → private conversation.

---

# STAGE 4: Executive AI Office Hours
Format:
- 60 minutes
- 8–12 leaders max
- No slides heavy
- Discussion-driven
- Focus: governance, ROI, real implementation
Goal: Surface real enterprise problems.
Outcome: Offer Readiness + ROI Sprint.

---

# STAGE 5: Paid Readiness + ROI Sprint
Low-friction paid diagnostic engagement.
Goal:
- Map AI readiness
- Identify ROI opportunities
- Deliver executive-level action plan
- Prove operational depth
This converts warm interest → serious client.

---

# STAGE 6: 90-Day Retainer
After sprint:
Offer 90-day structured roadmap engagement.
Deliverables:
- Governance framework
- Pilot build
- Executive alignment workshops
- Implementation plan

---

# KPI TRACKING
Track weekly:
- # executive RSVPs
- # executive follow-ups sent
- # Office Hours attendees
- # Sprint proposals sent
- # Retainers closed

No stage should be skipped.
