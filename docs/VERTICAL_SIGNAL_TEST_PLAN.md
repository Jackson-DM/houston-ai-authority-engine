# VERTICAL SIGNAL TEST PLAN
## 30-Day Industry Traction Experiment

Purpose: Identify which Houston industry vertical produces the strongest executive engagement and conversion velocity. 

**Duration:** 30 days
**Test Verticals:**
1. Energy & Industrials
2. Healthcare & Life Sciences
3. Legal / Professional Services

*We are not committing to a wedge yet. We are collecting signal.*

---

# TEST STRUCTURE
Each vertical receives:
- 1 Executive Memo
- 3 LinkedIn Posts (derived from memo)
- 1 Office Hours theme mention (rotating emphasis)
- Targeted LinkedIn commenting toward that sector

**We track:** executive engagement, DMs, discovery calls, and sprint conversations.

- Office Hours attendance by industry

# DECISION RULE (Day 30)
The winning wedge is the vertical that shows:
- Highest executive engagement density
- Fastest movement to discovery call
- Most urgency in conversations
- Clear budget authority in attendees

**The winner becomes:** Primary messaging wedge for next 90 days. Others remain secondary.
