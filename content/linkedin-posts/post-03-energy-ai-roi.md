# LinkedIn Authority Post

## Title
If You're an Energy Executive Thinking About AI Right Now, Ignore Most of the Headlines

## Source Memo
Houston Energy AI Operating Model

## Industry
Energy

## Post
If you're an energy executive thinking about AI right now, ignore most of the headlines.

The real ROI is not where people think.

The highest-leverage AI deployments in energy today are not flashy.

They're operational.

Things like:

• predictive maintenance insights
• automated reporting synthesis
• engineering knowledge retrieval
• emissions data interpretation
• field workflow documentation

None of those replace engineers.

They remove friction from engineering work.

That's where leverage appears.

AI doesn't need to replace expertise to transform an industry.

It only needs to amplify it.

The companies that understand this will move faster than those chasing the next tool.
