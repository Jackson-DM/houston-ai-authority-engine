# LinkedIn Authority Post

## Title
The Hidden Risk of AI in Healthcare

## Source Memo
Houston Healthcare AI Governance

## Industry
Healthcare

## Post
The biggest risk of AI in healthcare isn't incorrect answers.

It's unstructured adoption.

Right now, across hospitals and healthcare networks, the same pattern is emerging:

• clinicians experimenting with AI tools
• administrators testing workflow automation
• research teams exploring new models

Individually, these experiments look promising.

Collectively, they create fragmentation.

Different teams using different tools.

Different assumptions about what AI should do.

Different interpretations of data.

Healthcare systems cannot operate on fragmented intelligence.

They require coherence.

The organizations leading in healthcare AI adoption are not the ones experimenting the most.

They are the ones building clear operating models for how AI integrates into clinical and operational workflows.

Structure creates trust.

Trust enables adoption.
