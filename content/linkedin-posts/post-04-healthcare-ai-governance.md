# LinkedIn Authority Post

## Title
The Wrong Question Healthcare Leaders Are Asking

## Source Memo
Houston Healthcare AI Governance

## Industry
Healthcare

## Post
Many healthcare organizations are asking the wrong question about AI.

They ask:

"Where can we deploy AI?"

But the real question is:

"Where can we deploy AI without introducing operational or clinical risk?"

Healthcare is one of the most complex operating environments in the world.

Every decision intersects with:

• patient safety
• clinical workflows
• regulatory oversight
• institutional trust

That means AI adoption cannot follow the same playbook as tech companies.

It requires a different starting point.

Not tools.

Governance.

The healthcare systems making the most progress with AI right now are doing something simple but powerful:

They define governance frameworks first.

Then they deploy AI inside those boundaries.

In healthcare, trust is infrastructure.

AI has to respect that.
